# Bondand-Term-Deposit-Portfolio
Shows the summary of Bonds and Term Deposits Portfolio movement.


# Business Objectives
Analyze the portfolio movement of Bonds and Term Deposits.
Observe the portfolio comparison of two consecutive days, along with their affecting reaons.
Analyze average semi-annual and annual average portfolio against established KPIs.
Monitor the digital share of the total portfolio to insure complience with establshed KPIs.


# Type of Customers
Physical


# Data Model
Dim Tables : Deposit_Dim_By_Date, Funds_Involved_in_Bonds_Dim_By_Date, Clients_Dim_By_Date, Securities_Issuance_Dim_By_Date
Fact Tables : Deposit_Fact, Funds_Involved_in_Bonds_Fact, view_Exchange_Rate
